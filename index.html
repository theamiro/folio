HAKI

HAKI, is Swahili for rights — an app for training children across Africa on their rights.

It takes children across 5 different modules that teaches them about their rights, how to protect their rights, how to identify child rights violations and what to do about it. It incorporates testing of children after each module/lesson to ensure knowledge transfer. A simple test containing 4 questions from the previous module is used and a grade is assigned.
